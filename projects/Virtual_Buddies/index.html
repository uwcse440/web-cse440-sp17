Virtual Buddies

Virtual experiences, real friendships.

Learn More

Take charge of your social life from the comfort of your sofa.

Virtual Buddies allows your to connect with nursing home residents, family, and friends in a virtual world. Explore new horizons, engage in fun activities, and regain your independence.

The Problem

Retirement home residents often struggle with social isolation and maintaining social connections, particularly those residents who struggle with mobility. However, lacking deep social connections can lead to a lower quality of life. While many retirement homes seek to address this need with scheduled events and social programs, residents facing physical impairments are restricted in the events they can attend and may be dependent on limited caretaking resources and therefore are often left behind.

Our Solution

Virtual Buddies addresses this problem by facilitating social interactions in VR space. With Virtual Buddies, residents are no longer restricted by caretaking resources or mobility and have the agency to socialize freely with other residents. Virtual Buddies prioritizes accessibility needs while facilitating social connections.
