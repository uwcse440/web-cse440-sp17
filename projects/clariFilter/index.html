Overview

Explaining our initial proposal and our solution

Facts:

In 2015, 975,000, about 10% of university going students were international, over that school year about 17,000 of those students were from one of the original 7 travel banned countries back in January.

Problem:

Navigating through immigration news and related tasks is very time consuming and at UW, for every 2000 students there is one advisor who is directly responsible to help them with these tasks, which is both very stressful for the student and for the advisor. These students come to the united states to learn more on a subject of their choice and deserve to do so with peace of mind outside of their daily work.

Solution:

This is where clariFilter comes in, by providing filtered news, curated by our advisors, along with up-to-date checklists for tasks, we aim to help the students focus on their education. With news that is to the point, curated and accompanied with videos highlighting the news cycles, clariFilter helps get your attention to news for school away from home.
