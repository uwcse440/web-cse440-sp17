Overview

Volunteers are the primary social contact for 65% of elderly clients who recieve meal deliveries. Many clients rely on template support services to meet basic needs. Clients may feel disempowered by a process over which they have no control and intimidated by automated systems. Furthermore, volunteers lack organizational tools and service organizations lack ways to gather aggregate feedback.

Elderly Clients

Elderly clients are unable to provide feedback about quality of service or special dietary needs

Volunteers

Volunteers would benefit from an organizational tool to collect client information in one place

Service Organizations

Service organizations lack the means to gather information about health care needs and diet restrictions

We provide a quick and easy way for clients to voice their opinion and note their needs

Volunteers can view a profile for every client and an overview for their route

Service organizations can receive up-to-date information for each client from the volunteer
